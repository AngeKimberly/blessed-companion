🔥 IN THE BEGINNING—GOD! 🔥

The Foundation of Everything Starts With Him

📖 "In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth." —Genesis 1:1 (ESV)

Before time, before space, before light pierced the darkness—there was GOD.

The Bible doesn’t open with theories, debates, or human wisdom. It starts with an unshakable declaration of divine sovereignty.

No explanations. No apologies. Just absolute, unchanging TRUTH.

🌌 Why Does This First Verse Matter?

Because how you start determines how you finish.

  • The universe didn’t begin by accident—it began with GOD.
  • Your life wasn’t an afterthought—it was designed with intention.
  • Your purpose isn’t random—it’s rooted in Him.

If the entire cosmos rests on this truth, shouldn’t your life?

🚀 The Power of Starting With God

Most people build their lives on shaky foundations—money, success, relationships, or luck. But the strongest lives are built on GOD.

  • Start your day with Him.
  • Begin your plans with Him.
  • Anchor your dreams in Him.

When God is first, everything else aligns.

🔥 WHY Didn’t God Create Humans First?

Because He doesn’t do things haphazardly! Anyways, before introducing humanity, He set the stage:

  • He spoke light into existence—because you were meant to walk in clarity, not confusion.
  • He separated the waters—because you were designed for order, not chaos.
  • He filled the earth with life—because you were never meant to lack.

You were His grand finale—the crown of creation.

💥 Two Life-Changing Lessons From Genesis 1:1

1️⃣ FOUNDATIONS MATTER

  • Skyscrapers don’t start at the top. They dig deep first.
  • Great empires aren’t built overnight. They’re established on solid ground.
  • If your life feels unstable, check your foundation.

✨ "Unless the Lord builds the house, those who build it labor in vain." —Psalm 127:1

2️⃣ PLANNING PRECEDES PURPOSE

God didn’t create humans and then figure out the world. He prepared everything FIRST.

  • Your destiny isn’t an accident.
  • Your provision was secured before you arrived.
  • Your steps were ordered before you took them.

So why rush into life without seeking His blueprint?

🔔 YOUR TURN: What’s Your Life Built On?

Is it…

  • 💵 Wealth? (It fades.)
  • 🎲 Luck? (It’s unreliable.)
  • 📚 Knowledge? (It has limits.)
  • 👥 People? (They change.)
  • 🙌 GOD? (He never fails.)

🔥 FINAL THOUGHT: Reset Your Beginning

If your life feels off-track, go back to the starting point.

"In the beginning—GOD."

Realign. Rebuild. Restore. Let Him be your FIRST WORD, your FIRST THOUGHT, your FIRST LOVE.

🚀 When God is your foundation, EVERYTHING changes.

✨ SHARE if you believe that EVERYTHING begins and ends with GOD! ✨
